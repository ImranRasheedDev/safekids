We believe our kids deserve an internet that helps them become their best selves.

Hi there!
I’m Aahil

I’m an entrepreneur and student at Thomas Jefferson High School.

The internet is such a powerful source for good, for learning and growth. Yet parents have to continuously worry that their kids will be exposed to less than ideal content.

I believe in a world that offers kids the Internet on training wheels. So they can grow, create, and learn without worry of seeing content that’s not age-appropriate. We’re so passionate about this mission. And we’re so glad you’re here.
